# Conventions

> Style, naming, and formatting rules for this project.

## Choose your conventions

This is a generic project. Define your conventions here:

- **Formatting** — What tool do you use? What are the settings?
- **Naming** — What case for functions, variables, classes, files?
- **Imports** — How are imports organized?
- **Errors** — How are errors handled?
- **Testing** — What test framework? What naming conventions?
- **Git** — What commit format? What branch naming?
